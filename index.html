Best Top 3 Movies

These movies are considered among the best in film history. Check them out!

Dune

Directed by: Denis Villeneuve

Dune is a Sci-fi film. What made me love Dune so much was the visuals. The cinematography was truly a piece of art. It is such a unique film. If you're a fan of classic Sci-fi films want Dune.

The Notebook

Directed by: Nick Cassavetes

The Notebook is also another iconic Romance/Comedy film. It was filmed in 2004 starring Ryan Gosling and Rachel McAdams. What made me love this film the most was the storyline. The film beautifully captured the love interest between the main characters.

Interstellar

Directed by: Christopher Nolan

I saved best for last. Interstellar is a masterpiece. This is another Sci-fi film that will take place in the future. This film did amazing in every category possible from the visuals, storyline, and everything you can want when watching a film.
